How to play Death Box

Guess whether the next card is higher or lower. If you’re wrong, you drink until the timer hits zero. Survive until there’s only one stack left.

warning

Before you start

  • This is a drinking game. Know your limits and drink responsibly.
  • Ace is low, just to be annoying.
  • If you pull a joker, you drink.
Endgame
When the deck runs out, stacks are merged and reshuffled. Your goal is to survive until only one stack remains.
sports_esports

On your turn

  1. 1 Choose one of the nine stacks.
  2. 2 Predict higher or lower.
  3. 3 Get three predictions right to end your turn.
If you’re wrong
  • The stack size becomes your drinking time.
  • A countdown starts — drink until it hits zero.
