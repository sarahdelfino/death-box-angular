Remember:

  • This is a drinking game. Know your limits and drink responsibly.
  • Ace is low, just to be annoying.
  • If you pull a joker, you always drink and the joker goes to the bottom of the stack.
  • When the deck runs out, stacks are merged and reshuffled to keep the game going. The goal is to survive until only one stack remains.

On Your Turn

  • Choose one of the nine face-up stacks.
  • Predict whether the next card will be higher or lower.
  • Get three predictions right to end your turn.

If You’re Wrong

  • The size of the stack becomes your drinking time.
  • A countdown starts — drink until it hits zero.

The Counters

  • They can help you out… or make it worse. Tap to speed up the drinking time.
