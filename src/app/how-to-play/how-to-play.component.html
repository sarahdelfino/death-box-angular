How to Play

You are given nine stacks of cards laid out face-up in a 3x3 grid.

On your turn, you must select a stack and predict whether the next card will register as higher or lower than the value currently displayed.

Survive three correct predictions and the program spares you. But a single failure triggers the anomaly.

The number of cards in your chosen stack becomes your sentence. A digital countdown begins, binding you in static until it reaches zero.

The clock drains only at the mercy of the others connected to the machine. They tap, each strike pushing the timer forward — perhaps rushing you to safety… or slowing the feed so you remain trapped in the signal.

Pray the deck does not corrupt your path.

Pray your friends do not corrupt your fate.
